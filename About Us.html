Welcome to IKEA FURNITURES and Partner of K&K Italian Craft.

We are passionate about exceptional design, high-quality craftsmanship, and the convenience of flat-pack furniture. While we are not affiliated with IKEA, we recognize their impact as the world’s leading furniture retailer and pioneers of functional, well-designed products

At our store, we offer a carefully curated selection of parallel imported IKEA products, including some of their most iconic and best-selling pieces, all stocked in Midrand, Johannesburg. These products are 100% genuine, sourced directly from IKEA retail stores overseas. However, since they are parallel imports, no warranties or guarantees from Inter IKEA Systems B.V. apply.

In addition to our IKEA selection, we take pride in offering our own exclusive range of uniquely designed, Italian-crafted furniture—bringing together the best of Scandinavian functionality and Italian elegance. With a strong commitment to quality and customer satisfaction, we have built a reputation for delivering outstanding products and service.
